FLYBOARD

FLYBOARD HIRE

Come and experience the thrill of the latest in extreme watersports, the flyboard. It is similar to the hoverboard where a special high pressure hose is attached to the jetski, and then the magic happens. With the impulse that the water provides you fly up to a height of 10m and get to feel like a super hero whilst pulling off some radical maneuvers.

We offer flyboard, hoverboard and jetpack rides, single or tandem.

We specialise in Flyboards and are an official representative of ZAPATA RACING products in Brazil.
